BUFF Рђћ Delete Your Account

We respect your right to delete your account and associated data. This page explains how to request deletion.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to the address below with the following information:

To: buff.parenting@gmail.com
Subject: BUFF Account Deletion Request
Include in email:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your name (as it appears in the app)
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted
­ЪЊД Send Deletion Request

What happens next

  • Your request will be processed within 30 days
  • All personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted, including:
    • Profile information (name, email, children's names)
    • All tasks, rewards, and progress data
    • Family relationships and history
  • Anonymous aggregated data may be retained for service improvement
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What is NOT deleted

  • Subscription/payment records required for tax and legal compliance (kept by Google Play / Apple / RevenueCat, not by BUFF)
  • Anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked to you

Questions?

For any questions about data deletion or your privacy rights, please read our Privacy Policy or contact us at buff.parenting@gmail.com.
